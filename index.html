Patti Mayonnaise

About Patti

Patricia "Patti" Mayonnaise, the main female character of Doug, is a native of Bluffington and lives with her father, Chad Mayonnaise. She generally has a positive attitude towards life, however, she gets very grumpy when she is hungry.

Patti's Characteristics

  • She is the love interest of Doug Funny
  • She has short, curly, yellow hair
  • She LOVES polka dots
  • She is great at sports, and loves ballet, but is an awful cook
